Retina Ready


Retina Ready display is a fairly new concept born from the minds at Apple. It is necessary because the standard screen size and resolution of mobile devices is not optimally designed for the human eye at an average viewing distance.

Non-Retina:



Leaves you with blurry and ugly images


Coding for retina ready display basically involves programming the site to double down its pixel to screen ratio when accessed on a device with an irregular screen resolution. This is done through code at the CSS and Javascript level and is a fixture on all GCI made websites.

Retina:



Keeps images crisp and clean so that your product looks great!

Speed Sells

Website load time is the biggest contributing factor that separates good sites from bad sites. Internet users have short attention spans and a lot of options at their fingertips, which means they can afford not to waste their valuable web time on load screens and unresponsive pages. In fact the majority of users today expect a page to load in two seconds and each second after that represents a dramatic decline in conversion rates. Speed is also a major contributing factor toward optimizing your site for search engines or SEO.

Seconds

  • A 1 second delay (or 3 secs of waiting) decreases customer satisfaction by about 16%.
  • 40% of internet users abandon a website that takes more than 3 seconds to load.
  • 44% of online shoppers will tell their friends about a bad experience online.

The sweet and simple of it is this: faster websites create happier users and longer stay times, which builds customer loyalty. Search engines aim to provide a positive experience for their users, so they stack these sites higher on various keyword result listings.

That's why at GCI speed is always on the forefront of our design strategy and it's all about efficiently maximizing the use of space on your website. We highlight key information and content, then provide off page outlets for specific details and secondary information. This strategy ensures that with us you get a consistently responsive website, designed to look great, draw in users, and be easy for search engines to crawl and fully index
