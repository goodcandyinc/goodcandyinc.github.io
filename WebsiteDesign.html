Mobile Friendly

Whenever and wherever your customers are when they are struck with the desire for your product or service is when and where they want to know about it. A hungry man stuck in traffic isn't going to wait to get home to seek out his dinner options when he can actively start the search right away from his phone. This is a key growing trend that we pay very much attention to.

All Good Candy designed websites function flawlessly and look great across the full range internet ready devices. This way we ensure that no matter how your customers are viewing your website they all get the same sweet, simple look and professional experience.

Retina Ready

We feel that one of the truest test of quality lay in the little things. Small Details, that many overlook entirely, but when done right and brought to light with clarity and confidence can make a world of difference.

Retina Ready display is one of these details. Basically the standard screen size and resolution of most mobile devices make it difficult for the human eye to distinguish individual pixels at an optimal viewing distancy, which results in poor quality images and less then optimal user experience. At Good Candy Inc. that's simply not what we're about So to fix this, all of our websites are designed as Retina Ready Pages.

Speed Rules

It's an old saying that applies to a myriad of media based scenarios but perhaps none better than website design. Simply put, on the web speed kills. It affects key factors such as conversion rates, bounce rates, customer loyalty, and helps determine where your site belongs in the almighty search engine rankings. The sweet and simple of it is this; faster websites create happier users and longer stay times, Google aims to provide a positive experience for its users, so it likes this and therefore stack those sites higher on various keyword searches. It's a symbiotic relationship that's born with speed, is maintained by quality, and ends in exposure. The controlled positive kind. The kind that drives business.

Ultimately here at the Good Candy inc. that is our goal. To drive your business. And so when it comes to speed our web design strategy is this: Lean and Mean. To us sweet and simple means efficiently maximizing the use of space on your website. We highlight what's important, the stuff that makes you unique, your edge. And once that's captured effectively, provide off page outlets for specific details and secondary information. This strategy ensures that with us you get a consistently responsive website, designed to look great, and make it easy for search engines to crawl and fully index. Together making a winning combination both for the customers you have and for the ones you want. That's the GCI advantage.
